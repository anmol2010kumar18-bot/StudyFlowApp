Help & User Manual

Everything you need to know about StudyFlow

🚀 Getting Started

  • Fill your Personal Details once — saved in your browser forever.
  • When you return, StudyFlow loads automatically — no login needed.
  • Click Reset & Start Over in the sidebar to clear all data.
💡 New users start with zero data — all stats, streaks, XP and hours are earned by you.

✅ Tasks & Syllabus

  • Add tasks with a Subject tag — links them to your exams.
  • When you add an Exam, enter its Syllabus topics.
  • As you complete tasks for that subject, the Syllabus % rises automatically.
💡 More tasks completed per subject = higher syllabus completion.

⏱ Study Timer

  • Pomodoro Timer — 25 min focus, 5 min break.
  • Every completed session adds to your Total Study Hours.
  • Hours appear in Insights and Dashboard chart.

📅 Schedule Builder

  • Click Auto-Schedule to generate a smart timetable.
  • Click ✕ on any block to remove it.
  • Transport users get bus blocks added automatically.

📊 Insights (Live Data)

  • All charts update as you complete tasks and Pomodoro sessions.
  • Weekly chart = actual hours from your timer sessions.
  • Weak spots = subjects with most pending tasks.

🤖 AI Study Planner

  • Uses your live data: exams, tasks, hours, subjects, commute.
  • Ask for a study plan, exam strategy, or daily schedule.
  • Use Quick Prompts for common requests.

🏆 XP & Leaderboard

  • XP is calculated from real activity: tasks, sessions, streaks.
  • Streak increases when you log at least 1 Pomodoro per day.
  • Badges are earned automatically at milestones.

💾 Data & Privacy

  • All data stored in your browser's localStorage.
  • Nothing is sent to any server — 100% private.
  • Use Reset & Start Over to clear all data intentionally.
💡 To transfer data, use Chrome Sync or Firefox Sync.
